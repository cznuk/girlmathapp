Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 7, 2026

Chase Kunz ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Purchase details you enter (item names, prices, discounts, credits, expected usage duration)
  • Any additional context you provide for justifications

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • App usage analytics (features used, session duration)
  • Subscription status

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Generate personalized purchase justifications using AI
  • Store your calculation history (for premium subscribers)
  • Sync your data across devices via iCloud (for premium subscribers)
  • Process subscription payments through Apple
  • Improve our app and services

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

OpenAI: We send your purchase details (item name, price, discounts, and context) to OpenAI's API to generate justifications. This data is processed according to OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

Apple: We use Apple's services for:

  • iCloud sync (your calculation history is stored in your personal iCloud account)
  • In-app purchases and subscription management
  • App analytics

Data Storage

Local Storage: Your calculation history is stored locally on your device using Core Data.

iCloud Sync: For premium subscribers, calculation history syncs across your devices using your personal iCloud account. This data is encrypted and stored by Apple according to their privacy practices.

We do not store your personal data on our servers.

Data Retention

Your calculation history is retained on your device and iCloud account until you delete the app or clear your data. You can delete individual calculations or all data at any time within the app.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data within the app
  • Delete your calculation history
  • Disable iCloud sync in your device settings
  • Request deletion of any data we may have

Children's Privacy

Girl Math is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
