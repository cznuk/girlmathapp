Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 7, 2026

Chase Kunz ("we", "our", or "us") built Girl Math as a privacy-focused app. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our mobile application.

Our Approach to Privacy

We do not collect or store your personal data on our servers. Girl Math is designed to keep your information on your device and under your control.

Data You Enter

When you use Girl Math, you enter purchase details such as:

  • Item names
  • Prices, discounts, and credits
  • Expected usage duration
  • Any additional context

This information is:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Synced via your iCloud account (for premium subscribers) - we do not have access to your iCloud data
  • Sent to OpenAI to generate your justification (see below)

Third-Party Services

OpenAI: When you request a justification, the purchase details you entered are sent to OpenAI's API to generate the response. This data is processed according to OpenAI's Privacy Policy. We do not store this data ourselves.

Apple: If you subscribe to Girl Math Premium, your subscription is managed entirely by Apple through your Apple ID. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's standard App Store analytics may apply per their privacy practices.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or have access to:

  • Your name, email, or contact information
  • Your location
  • Your device identifiers
  • Your calculation history (it stays on your device/iCloud)
  • Your payment or financial information

Your Control

Since your data is stored locally on your device:

  • Delete the app to remove all local data
  • Manage iCloud sync in your device's Settings
  • Your data is never on our servers, so there's nothing for us to delete

Children's Privacy

Girl Math is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
